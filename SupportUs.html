We couldn't do our activities without people supporting us.
We need your help! We know there are lots of different good couses to take part in but let's get involve in our society too!
You can support young people who want to travel, broaden their horizons and learn about the world's diversity.

Let's check how to support us!

Buy a postcard

Whenever we travel to different places we would like to send postcards to our friends and supporters. That way of support is easy and suitable for both parties. You give as a donation and we send you a nice card as a thank you gift. Our society travels all the time to variety of countries and regions. Hence, our postcards will enrich every collection. You can send a donation to our bank account (contact us in private message) or via Student Union.

Share our activities on social media

You can help our society a lot by doing so little. If you see our new post, photo or like our activity, just share this with your friends on your social media profiles. Help us reach more people, tell them about our aims and inspire them. Just click "share".
Have you already done that? Would you like to hear about some benefits? Contact us and ask about becoming our ambassador.
